8.3.1.3   Font management for imported HTML

    For text to appear correctly in the output, all font variants must exist in DAS, including the font families and styles. For font styles, such as bold and italics, you must have those variants in DAS for each font family declared in the HTML. For example, if your imported HTML contains <p style="font-family: Arial; font-size: 12pt; font-weight: bold; font-style: italic;">, then you must have Arial 12-point bold italic in DAS.

    Note

    If you convert your imported HTML, any font-family information in your imported HTML content is lost when you convert.

    If you do not have the correct fonts in DAS, the engine will substitute another font and issue a message.
