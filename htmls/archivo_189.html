5.1.1.7   Swiss QR Code

    A Swiss QR Code barcode, also called the Swiss QR-bill barcode, is a Quick Response (QR) Code barcode that conforms to the technical requirements for Swiss Implementation Guidelines QR-Bill.

    Figure 5.8: Swiss QR Code barcode example

    A Swiss QR Code barcode alters a standard QR barcode as follows:

    • Modifies the size of the barcode to 46 x 46 millimeters

    • Places a graphic of the Swiss flag in the middle of the barcode

    • Checks to ensure the barcode has less than 997 bytes of data

    Note

    Swiss QR Code barcodes are available in Designer page designs only.
